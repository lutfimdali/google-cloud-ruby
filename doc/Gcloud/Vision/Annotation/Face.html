Overview

Face

The results of face detection.

See #faces and #face.

Examples:

require "gcloud"

gcloud = Gcloud.new
vision = gcloud.vision

image = vision.image "path/to/face.jpg"

face = image.face
face.confidence #=> 0.86162376
